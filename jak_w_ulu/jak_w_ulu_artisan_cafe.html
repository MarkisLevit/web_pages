Our Story

Inspired by the warmth of Scandinavian design and the richness of local traditions, JAK w ULU was born out of a passion for bringing people together over exceptional food and coffee. Every detail, from our sunlit corners to our artisan ceramics, is crafted to make you feel right at home.

We believe in the power of fresh, seasonal ingredients. Our bakers rise early to prepare homemade cakes, fresh pastries, and nourishing breakfasts that comfort the soul.
